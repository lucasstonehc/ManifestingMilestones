Meet me in the next chapter

Hello and welcome to Manifesting Milestones with Nicole!

I'm a Canadian who's been living in Brazil for some years now. As someone navigating life in a second language, I understand how frustrating it can be when you can’t express yourself the way you want to, especially when you're already at an intermediate level and craving more than just textbook English. Let’s face it, textbook English is helpful when you're a beginner, but you don’t want to sound robotic forever, do you?

I’m an online Communication and Self-Expression Coach, and I specialize in helping people who want to express themselves more naturally and confidently in real-life English. Something I’ve learned from living in Brazil is that language and culture are inseparable. It’s not just about sounding like a native; it’s about understanding natives when they speak.

Communication goes both ways. When you’re moving between cultures, you need more than grammar rules — you need cultural insight. That’s where I come in.

Being familiar with both Brazilian and Canadian cultures, I help bridge that gap. I understand the common challenges, misunderstandings, and unique expressions that can trip people up, and I help you navigate them with clarity and confidence.

This isn’t a traditional ESL class. There are no grammar drills or textbooks here. Instead, I offer a relaxed, supportive space where you can speak freely, make mistakes without judgment, and grow into the confident English speaker you want to be. Grammar correction happens gently and only when needed for clarity. The focus is on communication and self-expression, not perfection.

We know we’re doing well when we understand each other and conversation feels natural!

You’ll learn how native speakers really talk: idioms, slang, expressions, humour, and the flow of natural conversation. It’s not just about learning English… It's about using it to express you!

We’ll talk about your life, goals, culture, and challenges — and anything else you’d talk about with a trusted friend (with a bit more structure and professional guidance, of course!). Here, you will receive guidance on how to navigate through language, culture, and relationships.

Have a question you’re normally embarrassed to ask? Don’t be! You can run it by me first, in a safe space where you can experiment, learn, and feel prepared for the real world.

Whether you're looking to feel more natural in conversation, gain confidence, receive guidance, express yourself more clearly in social situations, or simply enjoy a deeper connection to the English-speaking world — I’m here to help. I bring patience, positivity, and personal experience of what it feels like to be in your shoes.

We’ll show the world who you are.

Together, we’ll do more than just introduce you to the world — because the world needs your voice.
